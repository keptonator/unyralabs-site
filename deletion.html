Privacy Choices & Data Deletion

Delete your documents

You can delete files directly in the app. Deleting removes them from local storage on your device.

Delete iCloud copies (if enabled)

If you enabled iCloud sync, you can manage/delete iCloud files via the Files app or iCloud settings on your device.

Request help or data-related questions

Email us at support@unyralabs.com and include “Privacy Request” in the subject.
